Our History

Founded in 2010, Fictional Co. began as a small startup with a big dream: to simplify everyday life with smart, creative technology. Over the years, we’ve grown into a global team of innovators and thinkers.

From our first app launch to expanding across continents, our journey has been about resilience, bold ideas, and building long-lasting relationships with our customers. A fictional company is a business entity that exists only in the realm of fiction, often appearing in books, movies, television shows, video games, or other creative works.
